FAQs

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How much does a website cost?
Every project is different. A basic business website starts around ₹15,000. E-commerce, custom apps, or complex platforms are quoted based on scope. We share a detailed, fixed-price proposal no surprises.
How long does it take to build a website?
A typical business website takes 2–4 weeks. Custom web applications take 6–12 weeks. Mobile apps typically take 8–14 weeks depending on complexity. We always give you a clear timeline upfront.
Do you work with businesses across all of UP?
Yes! We serve businesses across all of Uttar Pradesh Lucknow, Kanpur, Prayagraj, Varanasi, Agra, Noida, Mathura, and beyond. We also work with clients pan-India and internationally. Everything can be handled remotely, efficiently.
What happens after the website is launched?
We offer maintenance packages, hosting support, and ongoing growth services. We don't disappear after delivery we aim to be your long-term technology partner.
How does SEO actually work and how long does it take?
SEO is a long-term investment. You typically start seeing meaningful results in 60–120 days. We handle technical SEO, local SEO (Google Business Profile), content, and link-building. No black-hat tricks only strategies that last.
